About

Welcome to Tiny's Notepad

Poetry is a form of expression, Writing let's us get out of our feelings and get in touch with these feelings in another world, beautiful and amazing to us. Tiny's Notepad holds the thoughts to every emotion you feel; Whatever it is that you feel, you need an expression. That's what we are about at Tiny's Notepad, we express your feelings in words that you defintely can relate to.

Tiny's Notepad is a safe haven for thoughts that you didn't know you had; and even those you are aware of, all you need is expression; Tiny's Notepad is your outlet. We have a burning passion to write what we feel and we know that you feel it too. 15years of writing makes you that good; good enoug to reflect what you're feeling without asking. Trust Tiny's Notepad for the best content to express your thoughts!

To reach out to every person out there with a thought in mind and no words to express these thought. Tiny's Notepad is a collection of emotions relatable to someone, just find us.

Poetry can affect all generations, and make people consider anything from love to loss, indeed poetry does what little else can, it can inspire. The poet speaks to the reader intimately and exclusively giving you an insight into the inner workings of their minds, their ideas, their loves and hates.

Poetry is ordinary language raised to the Nth power. Poetry is boned with ideas, nerved and blooded with emotions, all held together by the delicate, tough skin of words. To be a poet is a condition not a profession, to be expressive is the best condition ever. At Tiny's Notepad, we love the condition of being able to minister with words and we do it from our hearts and minds, from the deepest part of our souls.

Tiny's Notepad is where you want to seek solace, it's where you want to go to when you want to be cocooned in a cloud of sweet words, it's where you want to be when you are enraged beyond control, it's where you should be when you are overwhelmed with love; whatever it is you're feeling Tiny's Notepad is your destination to expression.
